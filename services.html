Printing models you send us

If you have 3d model already designed, you send me your 3d model, and we arrange for price and time needed for model to be 3d printed. Time needed for model to be sent to you depends, but usually it takes around a week, for you to get model via post office. You can also pickup your model if you think it can get damaged during delivery.

Don't hesitate and write us an email with your idea

3d printing already designed objects

We have loads of already designed objects in our portfolio, with fixed price. It takes around a week for us to ship an object to you.

Check our portfolio for more info

Want to try 3d printing yourself?

Because 3d printers we use are open source, that means you can have 3d printer for yourself at home as-well for a reasonable price. Lots of parts needed for 3d printer can be 3d printed, other parts we buy for you, and ship it you, with all instructions needed for you to build it yourself.

Check our KITs page for more informations.
